Bringing joy through music

A familiar song can brighten the room.

Matt is a passionate live performer and multi-instrumentalist specializing in interactive entertainment for senior living communities, retirement facilities, and memory care centers throughout Orem and Utah Valley.

Every show is tailored for senior audiences with comfortable sound levels and friendly interaction that encourages residents to clap, smile, and sing along.

  • Live guitar and vocals
  • Professional sound equipment included
  • Serving senior communities throughout Utah Valley

The music

Golden oldies and Neil Diamond favorites.

Matt’s set includes classic pop, country, and rock songs from the 1950s, 60s, and 70s—music residents already know and love. Between songs, he connects directly with the audience and keeps the atmosphere positive and welcoming.

  • Sweet Caroline
  • Cracklin’ Rosie
  • Forever in Blue Jeans
  • Cherry, Cherry
  • I’m a Believer
  • More golden favorites
