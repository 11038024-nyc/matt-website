Bringing Joy Through Music

Welcome! Matt is a passionate live musical performer specializing in interactive, high-energy entertainment for senior living communities, retirement facilities, and memory care centers throughout Orem and the Utah Valley. With a warm vocal style and live instrumentation, Matt focuses on creating a joyful, nostalgic atmosphere where residents are encouraged to clap, smile, and sing along to the soundtracks of their lives.

Every performance is tailored to the unique needs of senior audiences, ensuring comfortable sound levels, high engagement, and a wonderful afternoon or evening of classic entertainment for your community's activities calendar.

The Music & Neil Diamond Tribute

Matt features an extensive repertoire of golden oldies and classic hits from the 1950s, 60s, and 70s. In particular, he specializes in a crowd-pleasing, authentic tribute to the timeless songs of Neil Diamond.

  • The Neil Diamond Setlist: Sing-along favorites including "Sweet Caroline," "Forever in Blue Jeans," "Cracklin' Rosie," "Cherry, Cherry," and "I'm a Believer."
  • Golden Oldies: A rich selection of beloved country, pop, and rock classics that spark wonderful memories.
  • Interactive Style: Matt connects directly with the audience between songs, sharing stories and spreading positive energy.
